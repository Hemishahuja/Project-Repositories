About Mantriq CNC

Precision machining solutions with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Mantriq CNC began with a simple mission: to provide Canadian manufacturers with access to high-quality CNC machining at competitive prices. Our founders, with decades of combined experience in precision manufacturing, recognized the need for a reliable offshore machining partner that could deliver consistent quality while handling all the complexities of international shipping and customs.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art facility with over 50 CNC machines, serving clients across industries including aerospace, medical, automotive, and industrial equipment. Our team of skilled machinists and engineers work closely with clients to ensure every part meets exact specifications.
